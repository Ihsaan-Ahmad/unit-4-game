Crystal Collector

Instructions

There are four crystals below. When the game starts, you will be given a random number. When you click on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score. To win, your score has to match the random number given at the beginning of the game. You will lose if the score goes beyond the given number. Good luck!
